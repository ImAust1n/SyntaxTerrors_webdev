ABOUT US

Moops is a travel platform designed to optimize user experiences by streamlining trip planning and enhancing every aspect of travel. At the core of Moops is MooGai, a smart, voice-activated chatbot that serves as a comprehensive travel assistant. MooGai helps users with essential services such as booking tickets, navigating through cities, managing public transport, and recommending local foods and cuisines. It also handles currency conversions, time zone adjustments, and language translation, ensuring a seamless and stress-free journey.

What sets Moops apart is its unique approach to user engagement. MooGai keeps users informed with real-time voice notifications, and encourages them to share their experiences by submitting feedback on places and restaurants. For every submission, users earn MooNey, a reward system that enhances their overall travel experience.

The feedback gathered contributes to Moops’ "Know-from-the-People" feature, which curates local insights, providing travelers with authentic, off-the-beaten-path recommendations directly from the people who know the area best. By blending practical tools with community-driven advice, Moops ensures a personalized, enriching travel experience, making it more than just a travel assistant – it’s a companion on your journey.
