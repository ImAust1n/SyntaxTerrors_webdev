Cultural Tips & Phrases

Welcome to Bangalore!

Helpful Phrases:

  • Hello: "Namaste"
  • Thank you: "Dhanyavaadhagalu"
  • Goodbye: "Hogibarthini"

Cultural Tips:

  • It is customary to greet people with "Namaste" and a slight bow.
  • Always remove your shoes before entering someone's house or a temple.
  • Be respectful when interacting with elders – it’s an important part of the culture.

Quick Tip: Bangalore

When visiting temples, be sure to dress modestly and remove your shoes before entering.
